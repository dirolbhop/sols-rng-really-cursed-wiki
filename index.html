The main objective of the game is to get Auras. Auras are obtained by rolling for them. They vary in design and chance, being much more detailed and impressive the rarer they get. Some auras are biome-specific, having a greater chance of obtaining that aura when a certain biome is present. Outside the biome, the aura can still be obtained, though at a rarer chance. This system is called Breakthrough. Specific auras can have mutations, which are different, albeit rarer versions of an aura, with a similar design although tweaked and recolored. These mutations usually have an identifier showing which aura it is mutated from (like Exotic · APEX), while others do not (like KROMAT1K). When an aura’s rarity is over 1,000, the aura will play a unique cutscene to go along with it. Also, it will appear in the chat. When an aura’s rarity is over 10,000, the aura will play a unique cutscene, involving a star. This will also appear in the chat. As of Era 8, there are a total of 102 auras in-game (121, if including developer exclusive). Of those auras: 96 of them are permanent 30 can be obtained via breakthroughs 25 are mutations (24, if including developer exclusive) 1 of them is craftable 8 of them are limited 27 of them are unobtainable/developer exclusive

effects in sols rng

Effects can be gained from various different means such as Potions and Gears/Items and can currently only boost the player's roll cooldown and luck. Effects can stack, meaning if the player uses a Lucky Potion twice, it will have double the duration but will keep the same +100% luck. biome,weather,time in sols rng

The Biomes/Weather/Time system is comprised of a pack of unique events that visually apply to the map and affect the chance of obtaining exclusive auras for that biome. The system operates similarly to the rolling system used by players to acquire auras, but instead, it is rolled every second. For instance, if the Starfall biome has a 1 in 7,500 chance of appearing, then every second, the system will "roll" to determine if the Starfall biome should appear or not. Currently, there are 6 biomes as of Era 7 (Normal, Hell, Starfall, Corruption, Null and Glitched) and 3 weathers (Windy, Snowy, and Rainy) in the game. Time is not included due to it being its own separate system. When a biome or weather spawns, players will be alerted in the chat, notifying them about which biome has spawned. Breakthrough Most auras (excluding Glitch & Oppression due to them being glitched biome exclusives) can also be obtained outside of their natural biome through a system known as breakthrough. Each biome has a respective multiplier, which increases the rarity of biome auras if their native biome is present. For example, rolling an ABYSSAL HUNTER with Rainy active would have its rarity at 1/100,000,000, while its rarity in any other biome is increased to 1/400,000,000. The biome multipliers are 3x for Windy, 3x for Snowy, 4x for Rainy, 5x for Corruption, 6x for Hell, 10x for Starfall, 1000x for Null, and none for Glitch (as it reunites every multiplier from every event). In Era 7, the definition of "breakthrough" was changed so that breakthroughs are now getting auras at a lower chance inside a specific biome.
